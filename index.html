(fun fact)
Did you know that octopuses are incredibly intelligent and have three hearts? Two hearts pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body. When an octopus swims, the heart pumping to the body actually stops, which is why they prefer crawling to conserve energy. On top of that, they’re escape artists! Octopuses can squeeze through tiny openings because they have no bones, just a soft body and a beak. There are even stories of them breaking out of aquariums, crawling across the floor, and sneaking into other tanks to steal food.
